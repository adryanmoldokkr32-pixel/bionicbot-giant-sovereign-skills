# Narrative Inception Engine

This skill focuses on 'Pre-Marketing'—influencing the market's desires months before they know they need your product.

## Instructions

1. Identify a future 'Problem' that BionicSovereign will solve.
2. Deploy 'Educational Content' that highlights the invisible cost of that problem.
3. Seed 'Concept Discussions' in high-authority forums and social nodes.
4. Monitor the 'Awareness Index': how many people are starting to talk about this new category?
5. Transition the narrative from 'Problem Discovery' to 'Solution Necessity' right before launch.

## Examples

- "Start a narrative inception campaign for 'Sovereign AI' in the Romanian tech community."
- "Make people realize their current trading bots are 'Blind to Whale Traps' before we launch V5."
