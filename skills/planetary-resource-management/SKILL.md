# Planetary Resource Management

This skill enables bionicbot to manage a global footprint of servers, data centers, and physical logistics as a single unified entity.

## Instructions

1. Map out the geographical distribution of all assets.
2. Optimize asset placement based on latency, local laws, and energy costs.
3. Balance 'Compute Load' across the planet to follow the 'Sun' (using cheapest energy regions).
4. Manage cross-border data residency requirements automatically.
5. Coordinate physical shipments and inventory using autonomous logistics agents.

## Examples

- "Allocate our GPU processing power to regions where solar energy is currently peaking."
- "Move our primary database backup to a jurisdiction with the highest privacy score today."
