# Autonomous Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

This skill focuses on the 'Brain' of a corporation, making real-time decisions on capital allocation and department focus.

## Instructions

1. Integrate with accounting, sales, and dev APIs to get a 360-degree view.
2. Automatically allocate budget to departments with the highest ROI/Growth.
3. Predict 'burn rate' and suggest automated cost-cutting before an emergency.
4. Manage 'In-house vs Outsourced' logic based on current speed and quality data.
5. Optimize the corporate hierarchy by identifying bottlenecks in decision-making.

## Examples

- "Analyze our Q1 data and re-allocate $50k from marketing to R&D if growth > 20%."
- "Which department is currently the bottleneck for our product launch?"
