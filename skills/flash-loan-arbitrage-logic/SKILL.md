# Flash-Loan Arbitrage Logic

This skill focuses on the DeFi mechanic of borrowing millions, trading, and repaying in a single block.

## Instructions

1. Identify price discrepancies across decentralized exchanges (DEXs) like Uniswap and Curve.
2. Write or optimize a Smart Contract for the flash loan execution.
3. Calculate gas costs vs. potential profit spread to ensure positive ROI.
4. Implement 'Slippage Protection' to prevent trade failure.
5. Simulate the transaction on a testnet before mainnet execution.

## Examples

- "Find a flash-loan opportunity for DAI between Aave and Sushiswap."
- "Design a multi-hop arbitrage path for ETH with a $1M flash loan."
