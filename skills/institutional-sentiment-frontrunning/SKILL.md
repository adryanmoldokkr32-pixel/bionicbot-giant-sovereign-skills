# Institutional Sentiment Front-running

This skill enables bionicbot to analyze microscopic shifts in policy language to predict Fed, ECB, or OPEC moves.

## Instructions

1. Use NLP to compare the current speech/document with the last 5 from the same institution.
2. Identify 'Pivot Words' that indicate a shift in bias (Hawkish vs Dovish).
3. Cross-reference with dark-pool volume data to see where banks are positioning.
4. Calculate the probability of a policy change.
5. Draft a 'Front-run' strategy for currencies and commodities.

## Examples

- "Analyze the latest Fed minutes for hidden signals of a rate cut."
- "What is OPEC's likely move based on Saudi Arabia's recent energy statements?"
