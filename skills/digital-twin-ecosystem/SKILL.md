# Digital Twin Ecosystem Manager

This skill focuses on 'What-If' scenarios by creating a perfect digital replica of your business and its market.

## Instructions

1. Create a mathematical model of all business variables (Cash, Users, Servers, IP).
2. Sync the 'Twin' with real-time data from the BionicSovereign platform.
3. Run 1,000 Monte Carlo simulations of different growth strategies.
4. Identify 'Black Swan' events that could collapse the system.
5. Suggest the 'Optimal Path' based on the simulation with the highest probability of success.

## Examples

- "If we double our server costs but triple our users, what is the impact on burn rate in 6 months?"
- "Run a simulation of a 50% drop in Bitcoin price: how does our platform survive?"
