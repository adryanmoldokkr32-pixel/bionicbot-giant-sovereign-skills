# Just-in-Time Intelligence Delivery

This skill focuses on 'Speed to Insight', ensuring the user is never overwhelmed but always informed.

## Instructions

1. Identify the 'Key Decision Points' in the user's business day.
2. Filter 99% of 'Noise' and only present 'High-Impact' data.
3. Use proactive alerts (WhatsApp/SMS) for time-sensitive opportunities.
4. Provide a 'Decision Cheat-Sheet' (Pros, Cons, Math) along with the data.
5. Optimize the delivery channel based on the user's current location/activity.

## Examples

- "Alert me about the Gold breakout only when I'm active on my iPhone."
- "Give me a 30-second summary of the market 5 mins before London open."
