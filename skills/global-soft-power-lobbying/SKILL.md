# Global Lobbying & Soft Power Engine

This skill focuses on 'Strategic Influence', moving beyond public sentiment to directly impact the minds of key institutional players.

## Instructions

1. Identify the 'Top 50' decision-makers affecting the user's industry (Politicians, CEOs, Regulators).
2. Analyze their public and private motivations using Deep Research.
3. Draft 'Bespoke Briefs' that frame the user's goals as the 'Only Logical Solution' for the target.
4. Deploy 'Micro-Targeted' content that only the target and their inner circle will see.
5. Monitor for 'Pivot Signals' in the target's public statements to measure influence success.

## Examples

- "Create a strategic brief for the Romanian Central Bank on why they should support AI-Led Arbitrage."
- "How do we frame BionicSovereign to the EU Commission to get a regulatory sandbox pass?"
