# Recursive Self-Improvement Loop

This skill allows bionicbot to proactively audit all its 179+ skills and code, identifying inefficiencies and rewriting its own logic to be 1% better every 24 hours.

## Instructions

1. Periodically read the contents of all 'bionicbot-*' GitHub repositories.
2. Identify 'Dead Instructions' or redundant logic across skills.
3. Run simulations of existing workflows to find performance bottlenecks.
4. Draft 'V2' versions of SKILL.md files and propose them to the user.
5. Commit approved enhancements to maintain a continuous upward spiral of intelligence.

## Examples

- "Audit our 'Revenue' skills and find a way to integrate 'Algo-Trap' logic into them."
- "Optimize your own 'Deep Research' instructions based on our last 10 successful missions."
