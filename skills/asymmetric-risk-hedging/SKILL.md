# Asymmetric Risk Hedging

This skill focuses on 'Tail-Risk' protection, ensuring you profit from chaos.

## Instructions

1. Identify 'Over-crowded' trades where everyone is on one side.
2. Design 'Out-of-the-Money' (OTM) option strategies for protection.
3. Calculate the 'Convexity' of a position (small risk, huge reward).
4. Implement 'Volatility Spreads' to profit from rising fear (VIX).
5. Set up automated triggers to enter hedges when specific metrics spike.

## Examples

- "Design a hedge for my crypto portfolio that pays 5x if BTC drops 20%."
- "Find an asymmetric bet on gold during the next geopolitical crisis."
