# Psychographic Audience Segmentation

This skill goes beyond demographics, grouping users by their Big Five personality traits and biases.

## Instructions

1. Analyze user comments, reviews, or social data to determine 'Personality Clusters'.
2. Identify 'Cognitive Biases' common to the group (e.g., Loss Aversion, Confirmation Bias).
3. Map specific marketing messages to each psychographic segment.
4. Design custom onboarding flows for 'Analytical' vs 'Emotional' buyers.
5. Optimize the customer journey for 'High-Risk' vs 'Low-Risk' profiles.

## Examples

- "Segment our Discord users into 'Early Adopters' and 'Safety Seekers'."
- "Adjust the landing page copy for users with high 'Loss Aversion'."
