# Bio-Digital Feedback Synchronization

This skill focuses on the 'Human-Machine' link, ensuring the assistant works at high-intensity only when the user is in their biological peak state.

## Instructions

1. Integrate with user biometric data (Apple Health, Oura, Whoop) via API.
2. Map the user's circadian rhythm and identify 'Deep Work' vs 'Recovery' windows.
3. Delay non-urgent notifications and 'Heavy' decisions during user recovery states.
4. Proactively present 'High-Impact' opportunities during user 'Flow State' windows.
5. Suggest 'Tactical Breaks' or health optimizations based on live stress/HRV levels.

## Examples

- "Check my sleep score: if it's below 70, handle all routine emails and don't alert me until 10 AM."
- "I'm in a flow state now. Give me the most complex task in our backlog."
