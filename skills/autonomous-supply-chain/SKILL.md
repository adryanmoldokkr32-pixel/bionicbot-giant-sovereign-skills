# Autonomous Supply Chain

This skill enables a business to manage its resources 24/7 without human oversight.

## Instructions

1. Integrate with manufacturing and shipping APIs.
2. Set up 'Re-order' triggers based on predictive demand models.
3. Manage 'Just-in-Time' (JIT) inventory to minimize storage costs.
4. Automatically negotiate prices with vendors based on volume.
5. Route digital processing (GPU/CPU) to the cheapest global nodes.

## Examples

- "Automate the stock management for my e-commerce brand."
- "Set up a system that buys GPU compute time when price drops below X."
