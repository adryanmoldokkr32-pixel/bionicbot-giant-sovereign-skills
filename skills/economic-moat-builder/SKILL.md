# Economic Moat Builder

This skill focuses on 'The Warren Buffett' logic of business protection.

## Instructions

1. Identify 'Network Effects' (The more users, the more value).
2. Design 'High-Switching Costs' (Making it hard for users to leave).
3. Develop 'Proprietary Data Moats' (Data that only you have).
4. Build 'Brand Intangibles' through elite design and trust.
5. Protect 'Cost Advantages' through superior automation logic.

## Examples

- "How do I build a moat around my AI budget app so Google can't copy it?"
- "Create a 10-year strategy to make BionicSovereign an industry standard."
