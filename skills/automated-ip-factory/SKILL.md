# Automated Patent & IP Patenting Factory

This skill focuses on 'Intellectual Dominance'—protecting every innovation before anyone else can.

## Instructions

1. Continuously scan the project's codebase and research for 'Novel Concepts'.
2. Draft 'Provisional Patent' applications based on the identified concepts.
3. Perform 'Prior Art' searches to ensure the idea is truly unique.
4. Use AI to generate 10+ variations of a single idea to 'Flood' the IP space.
5. Manage the filing timeline and alerts for renewal and maintenance fees.

## Examples

- "Analyze our 'Algo-Trap' logic and draft a provisional patent application for it."
- "Find 5 novel ideas in our last 30 commits that we should protect as trade secrets."
