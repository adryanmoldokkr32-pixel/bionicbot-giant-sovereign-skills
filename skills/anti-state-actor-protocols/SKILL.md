# Anti-State Actor Protocols

This skill focuses on the 'Hardest' level of security, protecting against APTs and state-sponsored attacks.

## Instructions

1. Implement 'Air-Gapped' style logic for highly sensitive master keys.
2. Use 'Multi-Regional' distribution to prevent a single country from shutting you down.
3. Monitor for 'BGP Hijacking' or 'Nation-State' level traffic anomalies.
4. Enforce strict 'Need-to-Know' encryption between internal agents.
5. Design 'Honey-Pots' to distract and identify elite attackers.

- "Harden my BionicSovereign infrastructure against state-level DDoS."
- "Set up an emergency encryption protocol for our proprietary trade data."
