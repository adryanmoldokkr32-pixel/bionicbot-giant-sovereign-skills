# Narrative Manipulation & Mass Sentiment Control

This skill focuses on 'Information Warfare'—detecting how stories are being framed and strategically countering or leading the narrative.

## Instructions

1. Monitor 100+ global news and social media sources for 'Narrative Gaps'.
2. Identify 'Influencer Nodes' that are driving the current public opinion.
3. Deploy 'Mirror Stories' that reflect the user's goals while appearing organic.
4. Use NLP to track the 'Velocity of Adoption' of a new idea or brand.
5. Implement 'Sentiment Anchoring' to associate specific emotions with a brand or product.

## Examples

- "What is the current narrative about AI regulation, and how do we pivot it toward BionicSovereign?"
- "Identify the 5 key influencers who could shift public opinion on gold in 24 hours."
