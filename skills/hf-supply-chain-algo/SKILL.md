# Algorithmic High-Frequency Supply Chain

This skill focuses on 'Just-in-Time' (JIT) 2.0, using HFT logic to manage logistics at the speed of light.

## Instructions

1. Scrape shipping, weather, and geopolitical data to predict supply disruptions.
2. Use AI to automatically switch logistics providers within seconds of an incident.
3. Implement 'Dynamic Sourcing'—buy from the cheapest supplier at the exact millisecond needed.
4. Manage 'Edge Warehousing' logic to store goods where demand is predicted to spike.
5. Automate custom-clearance document prep for global trade speed.

## Examples

- "If a storm hits the Suez Canal, reroute our shipments from Shenzhen to Marseille instantly."
- "Find and book the cheapest cargo space for 100 units available in the next 2 hours."
