# Deep-Fake Detection & Defense

This skill proactively scans for and mitigates identity theft or brand damage via fake video/audio.

## Instructions

1. Monitor social media and news for 'High-Similarity' visual/audio assets.
2. Use AI analysis to detect artifacts in suspected deep-fake content.
3. Implement 'Digital Watermarking' or 'On-chain Verification' for official assets.
4. Draft rapid-response protocols for public relations in case of a breach.
5. Provide tools for users to verify the authenticity of a message/video.

## Examples

- "Scan the web for any fake videos using the BionicSovereign logo."
- "How do we verify my AI voice messages to prevent impersonation?"
