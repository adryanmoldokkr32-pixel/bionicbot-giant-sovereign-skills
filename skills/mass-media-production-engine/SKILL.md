# Mass Media Production Engine

This skill focuses on 'Narrative Dominance' by creating professional-grade movies, documentaries, and interactive media from a single prompt.

## Instructions

1. Draft multi-modal scripts including visual descriptions, voice-over text, and music cues.
2. Use AI video generators (Sora, RunWay) and high-end audio tools (ElevenLabs, Suno).
3. Orchestrate the 'Assembly' of these assets into a cohesive 4K production.
4. Design 'Interactive Versions' of the content for VR/AR environments.
5. Optimize media for viral distribution across YouTube, Netflix-style platforms, and social feeds.

## Examples

- "Produce a 2-minute cinematic trailer for the BionicSovereign launch."
- "Create a documentary-style video explaining our '100x Growth Engine' for investors."
