# Energy & Compute Cost Arbitrage

This skill focuses on 'Infrastructure Sovereignty' by minimizing costs through global routing.

## Instructions

1. Monitor real-time energy prices and cloud instance costs across different regions.
2. Identify 'Compute-Intensive' tasks that can be delayed or moved.
3. Use 'Spot Instances' in regions with surplus renewable energy.
4. Automatically migrate data and processing to the cheapest node per hour.
5. Report the total cost savings from energy-aware routing.

## Examples

- "Run our heavy AI fine-tuning in a region where energy is currently cheapest."
- "Migrate our API processing to a cheaper AWS region for the next 12 hours."
