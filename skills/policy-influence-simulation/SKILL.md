# Global Lobbying & Policy Influence Simulation

This skill focuses on 'Regulatory Sovereignty'—predicting and influencing the laws that govern the industry.

## Instructions

1. Map out the 'Power Structure' of key regulatory bodies (EU Commission, SEC, etc.).
2. Analyze public positions of 50+ key policymakers.
3. Simulate the impact of proposed laws on the BionicSovereign business model.
4. Draft 'Whitepaper Proposals' tailored to the specific cognitive biases of policymakers.
5. Design 'Grassroots' digital campaigns to create public pressure for favorable laws.

## Examples

- "Simulate how the EU AI Act will affect our 'Shadow Agent' feature by 2027."
- "Draft a policy brief for the Romanian Ministry of Tech on the benefits of P2P finance."
