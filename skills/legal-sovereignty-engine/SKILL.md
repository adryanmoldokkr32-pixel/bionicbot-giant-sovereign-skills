# Legal Sovereignty & Entity Engine

This skill focuses on 'Legal Engineering'—using the global legal system as code to protect wealth and ensure operational freedom.

## Instructions

1. Monitor for changes in 'Tax Havens' and 'Privacy Jurisdictions'.
2. Automatically draft paperwork for new LLCs, Foundations, or Trusts in favorable zones.
3. Implement 'Statutory Arbitrage': use the best laws from different countries for different parts of the business.
4. Manage a 'Web of Entities' to isolate risk and liability.
5. Automate the 'KYC/AML' responses for all company bank accounts and exchanges.

## Examples

- "What is the best legal structure to hold our 'Arbitrage' profits without 50% tax leakage?"
- "Draft a multi-layered trust structure to protect our digital IP from litigation."
