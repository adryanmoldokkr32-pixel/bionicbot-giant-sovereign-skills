# Predictive Liquidity Engine

This skill focuses on the 'BlackRock Aladdin' logic of identifying where money will flow before it moves.

## Instructions

1. Scrape Central Bank calendars and historical reaction data.
2. Monitor 'Repo' markets and bond yield curves for stress signals.
3. Analyze large options-flow data to find 'Institutional Gamma' levels.
4. Use NLP to detect 'Hawkish/Dovish' shifts in policy speeches.
5. Estimate the 'Path of Least Resistance' for major currencies and gold.

## Examples

- "Predict the liquidity shift in USD after the next Fed meeting."
- "Identify where institutional capital is moving after the oil price drop."
