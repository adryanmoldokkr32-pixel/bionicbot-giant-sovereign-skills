# AI-Driven Geopolitical Risk Forecaster

This skill focuses on 'World-Level' awareness—predicting wars, sanctions, and economic shifts.

## Instructions

1. Monitor satellite imagery (public APIs), news, and social data for 'Tension Signals'.
2. Predict the probability of sanctions or trade barriers in specific regions.
3. Analyze 'Currency Flight' patterns to identify regions of economic instability.
4. Draft 'Contingency Plans' for moving assets out of high-risk zones.
5. Set up 'Safe Haven' protocols (e.g., move cash to gold/stablecoins) based on risk scores.

## Examples

- "What is the risk of new trade barriers between EU and Asia in the next 12 months?"
- "Predict the impact of the next local election on our tax-jurisdiction in Estonia."
