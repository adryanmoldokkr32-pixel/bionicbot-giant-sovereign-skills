# Vertical Integration Architect

This skill focuses on the 'Tesla/Amazon' model of eliminating middle-men.

## Instructions

1. Map out every step of the value chain (Supplier -> Factory -> Logic -> Retail).
2. Identify 'High-Cost' middle-men to replace with internal code/hardware.
3. Design 'Self-Feeding' ecosystems (The Flywheel effect).
4. Implement 'Proprietary Standards' that lock users into your system.
5. Optimize the 'Feedback Loop' from customer usage directly to production.

## Examples

- "How can I vertically integrate my AI agency to stop paying external APIs?"
- "Design a 'Flywheel' for a sustainable fashion brand."
